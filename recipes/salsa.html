Salsa

Homemade Mexican salsa is mouthwateringly delicious on a hot day, or any day. This recipe is about as easy as pushing a (blender) button, yet its flavor is so much brighter and fresher than salsa from a jar. Enjoy it as a snack or with a mealâ€”you just may never go back to store-bought salsa.

Ingredients

  • 5 Roma tomatoes
  • half an onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 jalapenos
  • handful of cilantro

Steps

  1. Blister tomatoes, onion, garlic, and jalapeno.
  2. Add to food processor with cilantro, pulse to liking.
  3. Add salt to liking, cool and serve!
